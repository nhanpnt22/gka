4. TRANSFORMATION GROUP

Define transformation group:

𝒯 acting on frameworks F = (Φ, Θ)

Allowed transformations include:

(1) Affine transformations on outcome space:
 Φ → AΦ + b
 where A invertible

(2) Positive monotone reparameterizations:
 Φ → f(Φ), preserving order

(3) Scaling transformations:
 Φ → αΦ, α > 0

(4) Parameter reparameterizations:
 Θ → ψ(Θ), bijective

Properties:
- closure under composition
- existence of inverse
- measurability

ℱ := { [F] under ~ }
