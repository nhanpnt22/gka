0. GENERATIVE REALITY (GROUND TRUTH PROCESS — FINAL, STRICT FORM)
------------------------------------------------------------------

State–Outcome Transition:
(S_{t+1}, Y_t) ~ P_real,t(· | S_t, a_t, C_t)

Constraints (True, Unobserved):
C_t = C(S_t, a_t)

Observation Process:
O_t ~ g_t(· | S_t, Y_{t-k}, a_{t-k})

--------------------------------------------------

Where:

P_real,t:
- unknown, non-stationary, action-dependent generative process

C_t:
- true constraints (risk, liquidity, irreversibility, delay, resources)

Y_t:
- realized outcome(s) of interest (jointly generated with state transition)

g_t:
- observation function (partial, noisy, delayed, manipulable)

--------------------------------------------------

Core Properties:

1. Partial Observability:
S_t = (x_t, z_t)
- x_t: observed components
- z_t: latent components

2. Non-Stationarity (Regime Dynamics):
P_real,t ≠ P_real,t+1
- structural change with unobservable timing

3. Endogeneity (Action Feedback):
a_t → P_real,t+k
a_t → C_{t+k}

4. Observation Distortion & Delay:
O_t ≠ (S_t, Y_t)
- observations are partial, noisy, delayed, and manipulable
- outcome signal propagates with delay:
  Y_t → O_{t+k}

5. State Aliasing:
g_t(S_t^(1)) ≈ g_t(S_t^(2))
- distinct states may induce indistinguishable observations

6. Heavy-Tailed & Non-Ergodic Risk:
- outcomes dominated by rare, high-impact events
- time averages ≠ ensemble averages

7. Path Dependence & Irreversibility:
S_{t+1} ∈ ℛ(S_t, a_t, C_t)
- reachable states constrained by prior transitions and constraints

8. Constraint Evolution & Activation:
C_{t+k} = C(S_{t+k}, a_{t+k})
- constraints evolve endogenously and may bind nonlinearly and abruptly

9. Delayed & Asymmetric Feedback:
Δt_impact < Δt_feedback
- actions can cause damage before feedback is observed

10. Structural Uncertainty (Irreducible):
∄ P̂ such that P̂ = P_real,t
- exact representation of the true process is impossible

--------------------------------------------------

Survival-Critical Implications:

- S_t, C_t, and risk are systematically underestimated
- O_t is not ground truth and may mislead inference
- Y_t is only indirectly observed with delay and distortion
- P_real,t is non-stationary → model validity is temporary
- learning lags impact → actions can exceed understanding
- constraint violations are nonlinear and potentially irreversible

--------------------------------------------------

Output (Interface to Higher Levels):

- latent state: S_t
- outcomes: Y_t
- observations: O_t
- true constraints: C_t
- structural signals: regime shifts, instability, constraint activation

--------------------------------------------------

One-Line Essence:

An unknown, non-stationary, and action-dependent generative process produces states, constraints, and outcomes that are only partially and delay-distorted through observations—under heavy-tailed risk, irreversible dynamics, and endogenously evolving constraints—making exact representation impossible and enforcing persistent uncertainty.
