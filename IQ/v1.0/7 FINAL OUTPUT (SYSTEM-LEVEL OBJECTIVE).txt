FINAL OUTPUT (SYSTEM-LEVEL OBJECTIVE)
------------------------------------------------------------------

A system that:

- survives under uncertainty, delay, and irreversible risk
- enforces strict probabilistic constraints to avoid non-recoverable loss
- preserves optionality by maintaining future feasible action space
- adapts continuously through learning under noisy, delayed, and biased feedback
- improves calibration between predicted and realized outcomes over time
- scales exposure only with validated understanding and confidence
- detects and responds to instability before outcome deterioration
- remains robust under model error, state uncertainty, and regime change

--------------------------------------------------

Validity Conditions (Over Time):

The system is valid if:

- survival is maintained (no irreversible loss)
- optionality is preserved or expanded
- calibration error decreases or remains controlled
- realized outcomes improve on a risk-adjusted basis
- adaptation speed ≥ environment change × exposure

Else:
→ system invalid → must reduce exposure, reset, or redesign

--------------------------------------------------

One-Line Essence:

A survival-first adaptive system that operates under irreducible uncertainty—preserving optionality, controlling irreversible risk, and compounding performance through calibrated learning and disciplined exposure aligned with evolving reality.
